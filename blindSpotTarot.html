Blind Spot Tarot Reading

This tarot spread is designed to help you uncover and understand your blind spots — those aspects of yourself or your situation that you may not be fully aware of. By revealing these hidden influences, the reading aims to provide clarity and guidance for personal growth and decision-making.

This card reflects the truth you are aware of — what you consciously acknowledge in your life. It shows your current perspective and the patterns you can clearly identify.

This card reveals what you are overlooking, either out of fear, habit, or denial. It highlights hidden influences or truths that affect your choices without your awareness.
