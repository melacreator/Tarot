Decision Tarot Reading

This tarot spread is designed to help you explore a significant decision by examining two possible paths. Each path is represented by a series of cards that reveal motivations, challenges, and potential outcomes. This reading aims to provide clarity and insight to support your decision-making process.

1 A

This card reveals the motivation behind choosing Path A. It shows the desire, need, fear, or emotional trigger that makes this option feel appealing at this time.

2 A

This card shows what you must invest or sacrifice if you follow Path A. It may involve time, patience, change, responsibility, or letting go of something familiar.

3 A

This card describes the potential outcome of choosing Path A — what you may gain, learn, achieve, or confront along the way.

1 B

This card explains the pull toward Path B. It may show logic, safety, influence from others, or a promise of stability that shapes your choice.

2 B

This card reveals what Path B requires from you — effort, change, responsibility, courage, or restraint.

3 B

This card shows what may unfold if you choose Path B — the emotional, practical, and spiritual consequences of remaining or moving in this direction.
